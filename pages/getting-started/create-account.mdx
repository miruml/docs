This getting started guide will walk through the setup and usage of Miru to deploy configurations to a sample application. This guide assumes you've already read the [core concepts](/pages/core-concepts/overview) and are familiar with the [vocabulary](/pages/vocabulary).

## Create an Account
To create an account with Miru, navigate to [this page](https://configs.miruml.com) and click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner.
