## Update workspace name

To be able to update the workspace name, you must have one of the following roles:

- <Tooltip tip={ADMIN_TOOLTIP.tip} cta={ADMIN_TOOLTIP.cta} href={ADMIN_TOOLTIP.href}>Admin</Tooltip>
- <Tooltip tip={OWNER_TOOLTIP.tip} cta={OWNER_TOOLTIP.cta} href={OWNER_TOOLTIP.href}>Owner</Tooltip>

To update your workspace's name, navigate to the [workspace](https://app.mirurobotics.com/settings/workspace) page.

Edit the **Name** field and hit **Enter** on your keyboard.

<Frame>
  ![](https://assets.mirurobotics.com/docs/v03/images/workspaces/main.png)
</Frame>

## Set workspace logo

To set your workspace logo, navigate to the [workspace](https://app.mirurobotics.com/settings/workspace) page.

Click the current logo and select the image you want to use from the file system.

<Frame>
  ![](https://assets.mirurobotics.com/docs/v03/images/workspaces/set-logo-hover.png)
</Frame>

A dialog will appear, allowing you to crop the image.

<Frame>
  ![](https://assets.mirurobotics.com/docs/v03/images/workspaces/crop-logo.png)
</Frame>

Crop the image to your desired size, then click **Save** to set your workspace logo.

## Transfer ownership

To transfer ownership of your workspace to another member, you must be the current owner.

To begin, navigate to the [members](https://app.mirurobotics.com/settings/members) page. Click the ellipses (...) of the member you want to transfer ownership to and hit **Transfer ownership**.

<Frame>
  ![](https://assets.mirurobotics.com/docs/v03/images/users/members/dropdown.png)
</Frame>

<Warning>
  Transferring workspace ownership is a sensitive operation and cannot be undone. Please proceed with caution.
</Warning>
