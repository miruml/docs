## Properties
The following properties are used to identify and manage invites. 

<ParamField path="email">

<ImmutableBadge />

The email address of the invitee.

Examples: `john.doe@example.com`, `jane.smith@example.com`
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="expires_at">

<ImmutableBadge />

The date and time the invite expires. Invites are valid for seven days from the time they were created.

Examples: `2025-11-19T12:00:00Z`

</ParamField>

<ParamField path="role">

<MutableBadge />

The <Tooltip tip={ROLE_TOOLTIP.tip} cta={ROLE_TOOLTIP.cta} href={ROLE_TOOLTIP.href}>role</Tooltip> of the invitee when they join the workspace. Must be one of the following:
- `admin`
- `member`

Examples: `admin`, `member`

</ParamField>
