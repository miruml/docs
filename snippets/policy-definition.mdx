A **policy** is a set of **overrides** that hierarchically evaluate to a **Concrete Config**. Policies have two components:
1. A policy schema
2. A hierarchical set of overrides

A **Concrete Config** must follow a specific **Config Schema** when deployed to a client. **Config Schemas** define a policy that evaluates the clientâ€™s tags to determine the appropriate **Concrete Config** for deployment.
