## miru login

Login to Miru

### Synopsis

Login to Miru and authenticate your local CLI session.
		
This command will:
- Redirect you to Miru to authenticate your session
- Store your credentials locally
- Enable access to Miru services

```
miru login
```

### Examples

```
# Login
miru login
```
